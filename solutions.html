We insert ourselves into the businesses of each client to properly understand their business needs and provide efficient solutions through a wide range of services. We advise on business strategies, consult on corporate, project and transaction structuring, as well as financial and debt restructuring. We advise on balance sheet structure and optimum debt levels, dividend policies and on expansionary strategies. We also conduct valuations and risk modeling, feasibility studies, industry and competitor analysis, and forecasting and scenario analysis.

We consult and lead clients through identification, analysis and strategic decision-making of potential transactions. Assist in buy-sell transactions, carry out pre-deal evaluations, provide expert valuation analysis and advise on deal structuring and closing. We also coordinate due diligence requirements, typically involving financial, taxation, commercial, control, pension, compensation, legal, environmental and management information matters. We execute transaction on behalf of clients, prepare information memoranda, assist with negotiations and finally post-merger integration.

The expertise and background of our team enables us to proficiently advice and assist in debt sourcing. We consult on balance sheet structures and optimum debt levels, bond issuance, refinancing or restructuring of debt/facilities, preparation of debt information memoranda, negotiation of loan agreements, terms sheets and covenants, and arrangement of finance via project syndication, club or ECA facilities. We provide information on international ratings for major capital issues and prepare loan agreements, including terms and conditions, covenants and deal structures on behalf of our clients.
