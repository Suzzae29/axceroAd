Axcero Limited, trading as Axcero Advisors was set up in 2014 as a mid-market West African focused Corporate Advisory and Consulting firm dedicated to serving the needs of leading companies with significant potential in the sub-region for growth and scale.


Supported by deep industry-specific credentials. Axcero professionals have served not only entrepreneurs but also industry leaders, private equity forms and leaders acrosss Africa. We deliver our senior executives to each transaction and pride ourselves in handholding and leading business owners, management teams, investors and lenders through highly complex mission-critical transactions and advisory engagements.



Vision & Mission
Core Values

Our mission is to be the preferred Investment Banking and Strategic Consulting Practice in Sub-Saharan Africa (SSA) distinguished by

  • Strong operational and investment expertise
  • Joint Ventures
  • Focus on SSA, ex-South Africa
  • Acceleration of Deal Execution through deployment of strong project management skills
  • Delivering attractive returns and building SSA growth platforms

We adhere to a set of fundamental core values that shape our mission. These values represent what we stand for, how we do business and how we interact with our clients.

  • Speed: We operate with a guiding principle of accelerating business/deal processes to closing. It is the differentiating element that distinguishes us from our competitors.
  • Execution: We encourage teamwork, innovation, business creativity and continuous learning among our people, to help facilitate delivery of distinctive accelerated results and solutions.
  • Commitment:
  • Honest and Transparent communication : We aim at building trust and confidence through open and frequent communication with our clients.
  • Integrity and ethical work practices : We strive to uphold the highest and globally recognized professionallism and ethical standards.

Ben G. Barth,

Managing Partner

Ben has several years’ experience in Management Consulting, Private Equity, Investment Banking, and Commercial Banking across several geographic markets like USA, South Africa, Nigeria, Congo, Liberia, Sierra Leone and Togo. Prior to co-founding Axcero, Ben worked as Business Development Director for Jonah Capital and Group Finance Head, Chester Engineers, Africa.

Prior to that, Ben Barth has had a storied banking experience working in Senior Executive Positions with Standard Bank of South Africa, Citigroup Inc., New York, and Ecobank Ghana Limited. Ben holds a BSc. Degree in Business Administration from the University of Ghana, and a MBA Degree from the Harvard Business School. He serves as a non-executive director at First Atlantic Bank, Unique Life Insurance and a member of the Advisory Council of the Sam E. Jonah School of Business.
